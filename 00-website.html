Seven proven modules that take you from zero to hired — built from 250+ real medical sales interviews.

01

Build a resume that stands out to medical sales hiring managers — even without direct experience. Learn how to highlight transferable skills and quantify your impact.

Master strategic LinkedIn outreach. Learn who to contact, exactly what to say, and how to build relationships that turn into interviews before you ever apply.

Career Trajectory

Ryan started his career as a biology major working as a swim instructor — no sales background, no connections. He went on over 100 interviews in four months just to learn the patterns, then built a repeatable system from what he discovered.

The insight that changed everything: the questions never change — only the person asking them does. That led to the Core-4 Story Method™, a framework that helped him land 94% of every role he went after.

He went from $53K to $185K+ base salary in his first 6 years — and well beyond that since, ultimately landing in oncology — one of the most coveted specialties in medical sales — in a role that required 7–10 years of experience he did not have. $185K was just the beginning. He got there because of how he interviewed.

Today he's coached hundreds of career changers and recent grads into medical sales — most landing their first role within 30 days of completing the program.
