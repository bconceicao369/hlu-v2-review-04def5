V2 Post-Call Email Sequence — "Booked but Not Enrolled Yet"

Purpose: Follow up with people who booked a strategy call but haven't enrolled. Job: get them to enroll (send them to the post-call checkout) or rebook if they fell off. Pairs with the post-registration nurture (email-sequence-v2-bookacall.md) and the tier-aware welcome emails (already live, fire on purchase).

Trigger logic — 100% AUTOMATIC (no tagging from Ryan)

GHL already knows the two things that matter, automatically:

  • Booked a call? → the appointment + call-booked tag is created when they book. Automatic.
  • Bought? → the purchased-core / purchased-resume / purchased-coaching tag fires on payment. Automatic.

So the whole sequence runs on pure logic:

Enter: contact has call-booked AND their appointment start time has passed (wait ~2 hours after) AND does NOT have any purchased-* tag.

Kill-switch (exit immediately): any purchased-* tag is added (they enrolled) → stop the sequence and let the Welcome sequence take over.

Ryan never has to tag anything. The only optional human input is below.

Optional refinement (only if you want it): no-show split

The one thing GHL can't know on its own is no-show vs. showed-but-didn't-buy. If Ryan marks the appointment "No-show" (one click in the calendar — and it's good to track show-rate anyway), you can branch:

  • No-show → lead with the rebook message (Email 1B below).
  • Showed, no purchase → lead with the enroll message (Email 1A below).

If Ryan doesn't mark it, the default Email 1 (combined) covers both cleanly. Recommendation: launch with the combined version, add the split later only if you want tighter copy.
